Tacos in Tangier – Generous & Flavorful

In Tangier, the taco has become much more than just a quick meal—it’s a true culinary passion. At Tramontana, we’ve perfected the art of the taco, a unique creation that blends Mediterranean flavors with modern cooking techniques. Located in Aida Village Complex on Avenue Moulay Rachid, we serve the best tacos in Tangier. Each taco is made with fresh tortillas, premium-quality meats, and house-made sauces that truly set us apart. Whether you’re a fan of classics or seeking original creations, our selection of tacos in Tangier will satisfy every craving.

Classic Tacos – The Must-Tries

Our classic tacos start with everyone’s favorites. The Chicken Tacos feature tender grilled chicken, fresh vegetables, melted cheese, and our signature sauce—all wrapped in a golden, crispy tortilla. The Cordon Bleu Tacos elevate the experience with breaded, cheese-stuffed chicken for an explosion of flavor in every bite. The Chicken Nuggets Tacos are perfect for crunch lovers, featuring house-made nuggets, special sauce, and generous toppings. Every taco is grilled to perfection—crispy outside, soft inside—making Tramontana one of Tangier’s top taco destinations.

Signature Creations – Tramontana Excellence

Our signature range pushes the boundaries of the traditional taco. The Minced Meat Tacos are generously filled with spiced ground beef, crisp vegetables, and our secret blend of three cheeses. The Mix Tacos combine multiple proteins for a flavor explosion—grilled chicken, minced meat, and crispy nuggets in one generous wrap. For seafood lovers, our Seafood Tacos are our pride: shrimp, calamari, and fresh fish of the day, seasoned with Mediterranean spices and drizzled with our house-made garlic-lemon sauce. These creations make Tramontana far more than just a fast-food spot in Tangier—it’s a destination for authentic taco lovers.

Fresh Ingredients – The Secret to a Great Taco

An excellent taco starts with excellent ingredients. At Tramontana, we use only fresh meat—never frozen. Our tortillas are made daily to guarantee perfect texture. Vegetables—tomatoes, lettuce, onions, peppers—come from local Tangier markets for maximum freshness. Our cheese is a special blend of three varieties that melt perfectly. Our house sauces are prepared daily using exclusive recipes: creamy white sauce, spicy Samurai sauce, and zesty Algerian sauce. This commitment to quality ensures every taco at Tramontana in Tangier is a delicious and authentic experience.

Unique Preparation – The Tramontana Technique

What sets our tacos apart is our unique preparation method. Each taco is carefully assembled: the tortilla is warmed, generously filled with meat, vegetables, and cheese, then rolled and grilled on a plancha. This final cooking step creates a golden, crispy shell that holds everything together while allowing the cheese to melt perfectly. We serve every taco piping hot, with crispy fries and extra sauce on the side. This attention to detail makes every taco at Tramontana in Tangier a memorable experience.

Taco Delivery in Tangier – Fast, Hot & Crispy

Craving a taco without leaving your couch? Tramontana delivers all our tacos straight to your door in Tangier via Glovo. Thanks to specially designed packaging that preserves heat and crispiness, your taco arrives just as perfect as in the restaurant: crispy tortilla, generous filling, melted cheese, and hot fries. Whether it’s a quick lunch, family dinner, or friends’ night in, order in just a few clicks on the Glovo app and enjoy the best taco delivery in Tangier.
